LEARNING LIKELIHOODS WITH CONDITIONAL NORMALIZING FLOWS

Background

Standard NF:

Let $\mathcal{Y}$ and $\mathcal{Z}$ be two spaces of equal dimensions, define a map $f_\phi : \mathcal{Y} \rightarrow \mathcal{Z}$. Given prior $p_\mathcal{Z}(z)$, we can infer $$ \int_{\Omega} dF_\mathcal{Z} = \int_{f_\phi^{-1}(\Omega)} dF_\mathcal{Y} $$ for any $\Omega \in \mathcal{F}_Z$.
